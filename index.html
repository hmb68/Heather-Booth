Hi, Guys! My name is Heather

Biology

I am a fourth-year undergraduate majoring in Biology, minoring in Spanish and Chemistry, and completing a certificate in the Conceptual Foundations of Medicine. In the future, I hope to attend medical school and partner with the Air Force Health Professions Scholarship Program (HPSP) to pursue a career in trauma surgery/emergency medicine.

I love spending time outdoors either hiking, fishing, skiing or photographing scenery. I’m also an EMT and clinical lab specimen processor for UPMC.

Knowledge, Tools, and Skills

Although my knowledge of digital design is quite limited, I’m excited to apply my quick learning, flexibility, and attention to detail to grow and challenge myself throughout the semester. I really enjoy conducting research, interpreting data, and formulating solutions to complex problems. Since my approach to life is learning/thinking from as many perspectives as possible, gaining experience in this unfamiliar field will help me develop a more comprehensive understanding of the world.

I have some experience in composing digital art and sketches. I’m excited to learn how to use code as a creative tool and organize/present a topic in a way that is accessible to diverse audiences. I also hope to better understand videography/photography techniques.

Potential Project

I would like to create a social/political commentary on workplace conditions and miscommunication within the healthcare system. Working within the different branches (geriatric care, clinical research, emergency medicine) made me realize how disjointed/ineffective collaboration detrimentally impacts patient care and employee life quality. I plan on interviewing people from these different fields. I know at this moment my topic is very broad, but my goal is to pinpoint one specific issue and create a structured plan to solve it.

In the Future

Although I plan on attending medical school, my ultimate goal is to change the system (becoming a doctor and serving in the military would give me a complete/thorough understanding of the entire institution). Whether this is regarding healthcare, the socioeconomic sector, or the global climate crisis, I want to pinpoint issues and formulate/implement logical solutions. Some of my passions include eliminating racist technology from practice, mistreatment of the elderly, and discrimination against the LGBTQ community. I want this project to be a stepping stone to help me figure out:

1. What I am truly passionate about

2. What I want to fix

3. How I can make change/help as many people as possible
